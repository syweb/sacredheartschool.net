Directions to the Tour

The tour starts on 38th Street between M and Folsom Streets. Look for the posted signs!

The tour will wind its way through East Sacramento. You may start the home tour at any home, and parking is available outside all the tour homes and at the Holiday Cafe & Boutique, located at the school.


From Auburn/Roseville
Take the J Street exit off of the Capital City Freeway.
Head east to 38th Street and turn right for house #1. Or turn right on 39th Street for Boutique at the school.


From Placerville
Take Highway 50 to the 65th Street exit.
Turn right on 65th St.
Go one block to Folsom Boulevard and turn left.
Go to 38th St. and turn right.
The house will be on your left just before M Street.


From Stockton and Elk Grove
Take Highway 99 North to the H St. exit.
Head east (right) to 39th St. and turn right.
The school is on your right. Continue down 39th street, take a right on M Street and follow to 38th St., turn left and the house will be on your right.


From San Francisco
Take Highway 80 to the Capital City Freeway.
Take the H Street exit.
Head east (right) to 39th Street and turn right.
The school is on your right. Continue down 39th street, take a right on M Street and follow to 38th St., turn left and the house will be on your right.
