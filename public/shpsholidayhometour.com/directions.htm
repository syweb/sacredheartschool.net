Directions to the Tour

The tour starts on 43rd Street between J and M Streets. Look for the posted signs! The tour will wind its way through East Sacramento. You may start the tour at any home, and parking is available outside all the tour homes and at the Holiday Cafe and Gift Shop, located at Sacred Heart Parish School at 856 39th Street. Tickets can be purchased at the School and at the 43rd Street home.


From Auburn/Roseville
Take the J Street exit off of the Capital City Freeway. Head east (left) to 43rd Street and turn right for house #1. Or turn right on 39th Street for the Cafe and Gift Shop at the school.


From Placerville
Take Highway 50 to the 65th Street exit. Turn right on 65th St., Go one block to Folsom Boulevard and turn left. Go to 43rd St. and turn right. Cross over M and it will be on your left.


From Stockton and Elk Grove
Take Highway 99 North to the H St. exit. Head east (right) to 39th St. and turn right. The school is on your right. Continue down 39th street, take a left on J Street to 43rd St. and turn right.


From San Francisco
Take Highway 80 to the Capital City Freeway. Take the H Street exit. Head east (right) to 39th Street and turn right. The school is on your right. Continue down 39th street, take a left on J Street to 43rd St. and turn right.
