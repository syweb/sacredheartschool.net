Holiday Home Tour Ticket Sales

Welcome to the Sacred Heart Holiday Home Tour ticket store.

Tickets will be available November 1st. You can purchase tickets online, at the local retailer locations listed below and at the Sacred Heart School office. The school address is 856 39th Street, Sacramento, CA 95816.

Purchase tickets online now!

Will Call

All online tickets will be held at will-call. They can be picked up at the school during the home tour event hours.

**Please note there are special will call locations for Friday:

On Friday morning only, will-call will be located in the Mercy McMahon Terrace at 3865 J St. Will-call will move to Sacred Heart School, at 856 39th Street, at 12:30 pm.

9-12 - Mercy McMahon Terrace (3865 J Street)
12:30 - 7p - Sacred Heart School (856 39th Street)

Will call will remain at the school for the duration of the home tour weekend. Will-call tickets will only be available for pick-up at the school. Will-call tickets are not available for pick-up at any of the houses.

Ticket Prices

Ticket Prices are $30 through December 4th.
Ticket Prices are $35 beginning December 5th.

You may use your ticket for a one-time admittance to each house any time during the home tour hours.

*Children over the age of 1 must have their own ticket. Organizers predict another sellout again this year and suggest advance purchases when possible.

*Ticket sales are non-refundable. The home tour occurs rain or shine.

To Purchase Tickets on the Day of the Tour

On home tour days, tickets can be purchased at Sacred Heart School. The school is located at 856 39th Street. Cash or checks are accepted. They may also be purchased on the tour at the house located on 45th Street. Look for the signs!
