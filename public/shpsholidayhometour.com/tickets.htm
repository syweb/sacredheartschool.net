Holiday Home Tour Ticket Sales

Welcome to the Sacred Heart Holiday Home Tour ticket store.

Tickets will be available November 1st. You can purchase tickets online, at the local retail locations listed below and at the Sacred Heart School office. The school address is 856 39th Street, Sacramento, CA 95816.

On the weekend of the tour, tickets can be purchased at Sacred Heart School or the 4th home on the tour located on 41st Street. Both locations will accept cash and credit cards.


Will Call

All online tickets will be held at will call. They can be picked up at the school during the home tour event hours.

Will call will be located at Sacred Heart Parish School, at 856 39th Street, starting at 9:30 a.m., Friday (11/30) and Saturday (12/1) morning, and at 10:30am on Sunday (12/2).

Will call tickets will only be available for pick-up at the school. Will call tickets are not available for pick-up at any of the houses.

Ticket Prices

Ticket Prices are $30 through November 29th
Ticket Prices are $35 beginning November 30th

You may use your ticket for a one-time admittance to each house any time during the home tour hours.

*Children over the age of 1 must have their own ticket. Organizers predict another sellout again this year and suggest advance purchases when possible.

*Ticket sales are non-refundable. The home tour occurs rain or shine.

To Purchase Tickets During the Tour

On home tour days, tickets can be purchased at Sacred Heart School. The school is located at 856 39th Street. Cash, checks and credit cards are accepted. They may also be purchased on the tour at the house located on 41st Street. Look for the signs!
