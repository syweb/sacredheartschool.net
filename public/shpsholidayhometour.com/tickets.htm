Holiday Home Tour Ticket Sales

Welcome to the Sacred Heart Holiday Home Tour ticket store.

Will Call

On Friday morning only, will-call will be located in the Mercy McMahon Terrace at 3865 J St. Will-call will move to Sacred Heart School, at 856 39th Street, at 12:30 pm on Friday. Will-call will remain at the school through the weekend. Will-call tickets will only be available for pick-up at the school. They are not available for pick-up at any of the houses.

Ticket Prices

Ticket Prices are $25 through December 1st.
Ticket Prices are $30 beginning December 2nd.

You may use your ticket for a one-time admittance to each house any time during the tour weekend.

*Children over the age of 1 must have their own ticket. Organizers predict another sellout again this year and suggest advance purchases when possible.

To Purchase Tickets on the Day of the Tour

On tour days, tickets can be purchased at Sacred Heart School. The school is located at 856 39th Street. Cash, check or credit cards are accepted. They may also be purchased at house #1 located on 46th Street. Look for the signs!
