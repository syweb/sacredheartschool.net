Holiday Home Tour Ticket Sales

Welcome to the Sacred Heart Holiday Home Tour ticket store. We are pleased to announce that we now offer a "surcharge free" transaction via PayPal.

Online tickets will be held at will-call for pick up on tour days. On Friday morning only, will-call will be located in the Mercy McMahon Terrace at 3865 J St. Will call will move to Sacred Heart School, 856 39th Street, 12:30 pm. Boutique also open at 12:30 pm at Sacred Heart Parish School. Will call will remain at the school for the rest of the weekend. Purchase Tickets Now!

Ticket Prices

Ticket Prices are $25 through December 2nd.
Ticket Prices are $30 beginning December 3rd.

You may use your ticket for a one-time admittance to each house any time during the tour weekend.

*Children over the age of 1 must have their own ticket. Organizers predict another sellout again this year and suggest advance purchases when possible.

To Purchase Tickets on the Day of the Tour

On tour days, tickets can be purchased at the Sacred Heart school, next to the Holiday Boutique, located at Sacred Heart Parish School, 856 39th Street. Cash, check or credit card are accepted. They may also be purchased at house #1 located on 43rd Street between J and M Streets. Look for the signs!
