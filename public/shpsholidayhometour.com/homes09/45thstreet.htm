French Eclectic

This beautifully maintained large family home is a warm and welcoming retreat for the holidays, especially with the design touches of Carol Shellenberger of Holiday Home. Built in 1927, the homeowners purchased this home in 2002. Architect Dennis Greenbaun helped the homeowners maintain the original character of the home when 1000 square feet was added. What a wonderful holiday treat for the homeowners when they moved back into the home on Christmas Eve 2006.
