Classic European Revival

After purchasing this home in late January these lucky homeowners were able to move in after their renovation took only sixteen weeks to complete. Their renovation was mostly cosmetic due to the charm this Classic European Revival possesses. Built in 1922 this beautifully maintained large family home is a warm and welcoming retreat for the holidays especially with the design touches of Chris Merenda-Axtell, who was also the homeowner’s designer upon the purchase of the home.
