Grand Bungalow

Built in the 1920’s, this Grand Bungalow home presents some of the best loved aspects of a home you expect to find in East Sacramento. A Japanese Maple is first to greet you as the owners have provided a lovely walkway which encompasses the beauty of the tree. The wide steps of the front porch provide a straightforward symmetry with the home’s planters and roof. With the perfectly framed arched doors and clean lines in the landscape, there is an understated elegance that portends this comfortable grand bungalow.
