English Cottage

Just walking up the beautiful brick-lined pathway leading up to the original facade of this house makes you want to come in for a hot cup of tea. This beautiful English Cottage retains the original charm it’s architects envisioned when it was constructed in 1921. The owners meticulously recycled the original brickwork during a renovation a few years ago and did a magnificent job at keeping the warm cottage feeling inside and out.
