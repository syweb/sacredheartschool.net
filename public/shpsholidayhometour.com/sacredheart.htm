Sacred Heart Parish School
A Community of Learners & Believers

Welcome to our school's most ambitious fundraiser of the year organized by the parents of Sacred Heart. This is the Home Tour's 36th year, but we're proud to say our school has been around a lot longer.

For over 75 years, Sacred Heart Parish School has supported families in raising their children, and we are proud that our current student body includes third- and fourth-generation students. We started in the Parish Hall in 1934 with 68 students and two teachers. Today, we have our own campus, 320 students and 31 faculty and staff.

Sacred Heart Parish School has a proud tradition of excellence and has long enjoyed a reputation for being a leader among diocesan schools in academics and athletics. As a community of learners, we educate each child's whole heart, whole soul, whole mind, whole strength. As a community of believers, we grow together in our faith to love God and our neighbors.

Our credentialed teaching team respects that each student is gifted and that what we teach, and how we teach, empowers them to be successful. Our highly supportive and involved parents create a dynamic home/school partnership.
Your support helps us maintain quality in our curriculum, compensate our teachers fairly, and preserve our ministry as an affordable blessing for our families.

Have a blessed Christmas and a happy, peaceful New Year.
