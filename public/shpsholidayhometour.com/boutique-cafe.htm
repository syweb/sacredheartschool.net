Holiday Boutique & Café

The Holiday Boutique and Café are located at Sacred Heart Parish School at 856-39th Street.

Shop for one of a kind holiday gifts and dine in a convenient, festive atmosphere. Tour tickets are not required for admission to the Boutique & café - all are welcome.

The Holiday Café & Boutique, feature unique gifts & holiday merchandise selected exclusively for Sacred Heart by designers & style experts.

While you are there treat yourself to the renowned Home Tour café, featuring tasty sandwiches, fresh salads, toasty soups and gourmet desserts. Specialty coffee, tea, mulled cider, soda, wine and beer are also available.
