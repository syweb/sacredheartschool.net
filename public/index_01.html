Welcome to Sacred Heart Parish School

Sacred Heart Parish School serves the Sacred Heart Parish Community by providing a quality Catholic elementary education (K-8) that is based on the Gospel values of Jesus, and is dedicated to the development of the spiritual, intellectual, social-emotional and physical life of our students.

Welcome Note

At Sacred Heart Parish School, we believe in educating the whole child. In our care students will grow intellectually, spiritually, socially, emotionally and physically. We are a small, Catholic, neighborhood school. We recognize each and every student as a child of God with unique talents and gifts. At Sacred Heart, all children have the chance to fulfill their academic and social potential while learning and experiencing the richness of the Catholic faith through developing a relationship with a loving God. Our Catholic faith and values are reflected in every aspect of our school, from the lesson plans to the physical structure. When students enter school each morning, they are greeted by the words, "Jesus, meek and humble of heart, make our hearts like yours." These are more than just reminders; they are the basis of everything we teach.

Immunization Reminder

A whooping cough (pertussis) booster shot is required for students entering 7th and 8th grades. California law requires students to be immunized against pertussis. An immunization booster shot must be given after the child's tenth birthday. Even though the State of California has granted a 30 day extension to get vaccinated, please ensure your child is up-to-date with his/her shots prior to the beginning of school on August 22nd. Students not in compliance will NOT be allowed on campus after September 22. Please bring proof of vaccination to the school office.

Make Mine A Short Stack!

Our pancake breakfast is back by popular demand. Join us on Sunday, September 18th after 8:00am and 9:30am Mass for a delicious pancake breakfast, served in the school café. The cost is $4 for kids and $6 for adults. IHOP wishes their pancakes were this good.

Fall Festival

Planning is in full swing for the 70th annual Fall Festival on October 1st. Food! Entertainment! General merriment! Arrive on campus at 11:00am, leave at dusk. You do not want to miss this event.

Now would be the time to bring your donations of wine, bottled water and cases of soda to the school office. Entry forms for the lip sync and hula hoop contests are available in the office or from your class room representatives. Please submit your contest forms as soon as possible.

Now is also the time to order your custom Fall Festival t-shirts. Trendy and stylish, you will want to order a t-shirt for everyone in the family! The t-shirts are available in youth and adult sizes. Orders can be placed in the school office. Sacred Heart School students will be allowed to wear the Fall Festival t-shirts with their uniform bottoms the Friday before the festival.

For the latest, most up-to-date information about Fall Festival please visit us on Facebook

Or e-mail the Festival committee: shpsfallfestival@gmail.com
