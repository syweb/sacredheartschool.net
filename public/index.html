Welcome to Sacred Heart Parish School

Sacred Heart Parish School serves the Sacred Heart Parish Community by providing a quality Catholic elementary education (K-8) that is based on the Gospel values of Jesus, and is dedicated to the development of the spiritual, intellectual, social-emotional and physical life of our students.

Welcome Note

At Sacred Heart Parish School, we believe in educating the whole child. In our care students will grow intellectually, spiritually, socially, emotionally and physically. We are a small, Catholic, neighborhood school. We recognize each and every student as a child of God with unique talents and gifts. At Sacred Heart, all children have the chance to fulfill their academic and social potential while learning and experiencing the richness of the Catholic faith through developing a relationship with a loving God. Our Catholic faith and values are reflected in every aspect of our school, from the lesson plans to the physical structure. When students enter school each morning, they are greeted by the words, "Jesus, meek and humble of heart, make our hearts like yours." These are more than just reminders; they are the basis of everything we teach.

Welcome Back!

We’ve missed you and we can’t wait to start another exciting year. Here are a few news items for the start of the new school year:

CLASSES BEGIN

We will see you here on August 22nd at 8:00am, in uniform and ready to go. (Gentlemen, that means neat hair cuts for you, and de-feathered hair for the girls.) Students in grades 1 – 8 will follow a minimum day schedule on August 22-24, with dismissal at noon. Our regular full day schedule begins on Thursday, August 25.

Kinder families with students attending the morning session will arrive by 8:00am and will be dismissed at 11:15am during the first week of school. Students attending the afternoon session should arrive by 11:45am. They will be dismissed at 3:15pm. The arrival of the afternoon kinders may overlap with the departure of the older students on the first three days of school. Therefore, we strongly urge the kinder parents to park at the church and walk their children in to campus.

TRAFFIC FLOW

Traffic flow during the first few weeks of school is bound to be very congested around our campus. In an on-going attempt to be good neighbors, we continue to encourage you to park at the church or at the curb on the west side of 39th Street and walk in to the campus. We have been visited several times by the city traffic enforcement department regarding the backup of traffic on 39th Street.

if you drop your children off in the morning, please remember that the parking lot gates open at 7:30am. Classrooms open at 7:45am. You may not park in front of the dumpsters or in the striped loading zone area on the south side of the parking lot. This area is reserved for Mercy McMahon Terrace only. Parking is not allowed on the playground.

When classes are dismissed at noon, please DO NOT arrive to pick up your children before 12:00. The gates will not open until noon. Early arrival will result in a backup of traffic onto 39th Street. This may cause a potentially dangerous situation for those vehicles stopped on the surface streets.

Dismissal on regular school days is at 3:00pm for grades 1 – 8. Please DO NOT arrive on campus before that time. The parking lot gates will not open until the children are ready for pickup at 3:00. Early arrival will result in a backup of traffic on 39th street. In a continued effort to improve the efficiency of our pick up operation, we will be reversing the order of the pickup line. The younger grades will wait at the far end of the pickup lane by the bike racks. Older siblings should wait with their younger brothers and sisters at the far end of the lot. Thank you for your cooperation. As always, the safety and well being of the children is our first priority.

THE COFFEE IS ON

Parents are welcome to join us for coffee and company in the cafeteria at 8:00am on the first morning of school. Get reconnected with old friends or meet some new ones!

Immunization Reminder

A whooping cough (pertussis) booster shot is required for students entering 7th and 8th grades. California law requires students to be immunized against pertussis. An immunization booster shot must be given after the child’s tenth birthday. Even though the State of California has granted a 30 day extension to get vaccinated, please ensure your child is up-to-date with his/her shots prior to the beginning of school on August 22nd. Students not in compliance will NOT be allowed on campus after September 22. Please bring proof of vaccination to the school office.
