Altar Servers

Altar Servers

Our school enjoys a strong connection with Sacred Heart Parish. Many students choose to serve the parish as altar servers. The altar serving schedule is available through Beehively under the “files” tab

The contact list is distributed at school for the Sacred Heart School students and via e-mail for the CCD students.

The altar service coordinator is Mrs. Clark. She can be reached at the school office.

Quick reminders for altar servers

  • Arrive at church 15 minutes prior to Mass time.
  • Volunteer to substitute or trade with other servers when necessary.
  • Don’t forget to sign-in in the sacristy.
  • Hang your robes up neatly.
